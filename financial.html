What is AstroTA?

AstroTA (Astrological Technical Analysis) combines ancient timing wisdom with modern chart patterns

📈

Planetary Support/Resistance

Just as technical analysis identifies price levels where buyers or sellers step in, planetary positions create energetic support and resistance zones in the market.

🌙

Lunar Cycle Patterns

Markets often bottom near New Moons and peak near Full Moons. We track these cycles against your specific trading timeframe and asset class.

♄

Retrograde Reversals

When Mercury, Venus, or Mars turn retrograde or direct, markets frequently experience trend reversals or acceleration. Timing entries around these stations.

☀️

Eclipse Volatility

Solar and lunar eclipses create 2-week windows of heightened volatility. We identify when to trade aggressively vs. when to reduce position size.

♃

Jupiter Expansion

Jupiter cycles correlate with bull markets and sector rotations. We track Jupiter's sign changes to identify which industries will outperform.

♆

Saturn Contraction

Saturn transits mark periods of consolidation, correction, and bear markets. Essential for knowing when to raise cash and play defense.
