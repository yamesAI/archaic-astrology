Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Birth Data: Date, time, and location of birth (required for astrology readings)
  • Contact Information: Name and email address (for service delivery)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we never store full card details)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site (via analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Generating your astrology readings and calculations
  • Delivering services you've purchased
  • Communicating about your bookings
  • Sending monthly calendars (if subscribed)
  • Improving our website and services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Email Service: For delivering readings and newsletters
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data only

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing via Stripe
  • Limited access to birth data (only the astrologer)
  • Regular security reviews

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Birth Data Sensitivity

Birth data is considered sensitive personal information in astrology. We:

  • Never share your exact birth time publicly
  • Only use this data for your requested services
  • Store birth data securely and accessibly only to the astrologer
  • Retain records for 7 years (for legal and tax purposes)

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand usage. You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact: privacy@archaicyames.com
