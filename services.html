Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for a reading?

Have your exact birth time, date, and location ready. The more precise the time, the more accurate the reading. If you don't know your birth time, we can still work with a range or focus on techniques that don't require it.

What's the difference between traditional and modern astrology?

Traditional astrology (used here) is predictive and specific, rooted in techniques from Ptolemy through the Renaissance. Modern astrology often focuses on psychological interpretation. We use the methods that survived empires because they produced results.

How soon should I book my Saturn Return reading?

Ideally 3-6 months before your Return begins. This gives you time to prepare and understand what's coming. I only accept 12 Saturn Return clients per month to ensure quality preparation time.

Are readings recorded?

Yes, all sessions are recorded (audio) and provided to you within 24 hours. You'll also receive any charts, diagrams, or written summaries discussed during the reading.

What's your refund policy?

If you need to reschedule, please give 48 hours notice. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit the booking fee. If you're unsatisfied with your reading, contact me within 7 days and we'll discuss a resolution.

Do you offer payment plans?

For packages over $300, I offer a 2-payment plan (50% to book, 50% before the first session). Contact me to arrange this.
