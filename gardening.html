The Ancient Wisdom

For thousands of years, farmers planted by the moon. The waxing moon draws sap upward—perfect for above-ground crops. The waning moon sends energy to roots—ideal for tubers and bulbs.

Each zodiac sign rules a part of the plant: Aries the seeds, Taurus the roots, Gemini the flowers. Planting when the moon is in the right sign for your crop is the secret to abundant harvests.
