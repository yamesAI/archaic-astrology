Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we elect our wedding date?

Ideally 6-12 months before the wedding. This gives you time to secure venues and vendors for the optimal dates. Rush elections (under 3 months) have limited options.

What if we don't know our exact birth times?

Birth times are important but not absolutely required. We can work with a time range or focus on date-only techniques. The more precise the time, the more detailed the election.

Can you work with our venue's limited availability?

Yes. Provide us with the dates your venue has available, and we'll rank them from most to least auspicious. Sometimes compromise is necessary—we'll guide you to the best option within your constraints.

What's the difference between synastry and composite?

Synastry compares two charts (how you affect each other). Composite creates a new chart for the relationship itself (the entity you form together). Both are valuable for different insights.
